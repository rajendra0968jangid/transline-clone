Founder & Director

Siddharth Singh

Mr. Siddharth Singh, The Founder and Director of Techsimula is a visionary leader dedicated to transforming education by making advanced technological learning accessible and practical for all. With over a decade of expertise in modern technologies and a passion for innovation, he has spearheaded Techsimula’s mission to revolutionize lab setups globally, focusing on fields like AI, Machine Learning, IoT, Data Science, Drones, and IT solutions. His commitment to bridging the gap between theory and hands-on learning has empowered institutions worldwide, providing affordable and user-friendly tools that foster skill development and innovation. Driven by the belief in equal access to quality education, he is reshaping the future of learning, enabling students to unlock their potential and thrive in a technology-driven world.

Our Vision

"Empowering Industries, Enriching Lives."
The vision is to create a world where advanced technological learning is accessible to all, empowering students and educators to innovate and thrive. As Nelson Mandela said, "Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Techsimula, an initiative by Upflairs, is revolutionizing education by providing advanced lab setups with AI, Machine Learning, IoT, and other modern technologies to colleges and universities. It bridges the gap between theory and hands-on learning, offering affordable solutions that enhance student education. Techsimula empowers both educators and students with easy-to-use tools, ensuring equal access to quality labs even in resource-limited schools. By fostering innovation and skill development, Techsimula is transforming education and empowering institutions worldwide.
